DIGITAL MOTION X-RAYS

Advanced Imaging Solutions

SHIMADZU Digital Motion X-ray systems are powered by battery-powered electric motors that aid in movement as technicians propel the mobile units with less effort, ideal for patients who cannot be brought to the radiology department.

ALL-IN-ONE R/F SYSTEM

Comprehensive Radiographic/Fluoroscopy

Our R/F systems show continuous X-ray images on monitors, like an X-ray movie. The image is transmitted to a monitor so the movement of a body part or an instrument or contrast agent through the body can be seen in detail.

FUJIFILM PRIMA T2 CR

High-Speed Imaging Solution

The Fujifilm Prima T2 CR Machine is a table-top reader unit which boasts a world-class high processing speed of 73 IPs/hour, helping reduce patient waiting time and increasing examination workflow efficiency.

FDR SMART X

Digital Radiography Excellence

The FDR Smart X offers ceiling suspended X-ray tube configurations and floor-mounted options, providing flexible solutions for your imaging department with high image quality via Fujifilm's latest D-EVO II series DR panels.
