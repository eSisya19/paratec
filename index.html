Welcome to Paras Technology

About Us

Founded in November 2018, Paras Technology has built a strong reputation through refurbishing abandoned hospital equipment from Japanese, Chinese, and European manufacturers. This success has fostered excellent relationships with the Ministry of Health, public hospitals, and private healthcare facilities across the region.

Our company is managed by highly experienced engineers specializing in various fields including PACS/DICOM systems, electrical and electronic systems, maintenance, and modern digital medical equipment such as CT, MRI, Mammography, Ultrasound, Theatre Operating Lamps, Anesthesia Machines, Dental Chairs, and many other critical healthcare technologies.

What We Do

We supply, maintain, and service a wide range of electrical/electronic medical instruments and devices. Our expertise extends to radiation protection consultancy, and we provide comprehensive installation services for all types of medical equipment. Our goal is to ensure healthcare facilities have reliable, well-maintained technology to deliver the best patient care possible.
